About Creator

Gege Akutami is the author of Jujutsu Kaisen. It is a pen name and the author's real name is unknown. He was born in Iwate Prefecture on February 26, 1992. He began drawing manga by mimicking a friend, which inspired them to become a professional manga artist. His editors are Tatsuhiko Katayama and Junya Fukuda.
